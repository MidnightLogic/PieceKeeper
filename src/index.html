Camera Access Required

To scan QR codes securely, PieceKeeper needs temporary access to your camera. Your video feed is processed entirely offline and is never transmitted.

App Installation Required

PieceKeeper is a hardware-grade web vault. To ensure maximum device security and safe offline routing for hardware NFC readers, you must install this application to your device's Home Screen.

Please use your browser menu to "Add to Home Screen" or "Install App", then launch it from your device desktop.

NFC Hardware Access

PieceKeeper needs permission to access your device's NFC antenna to read and write cryptographic shares.
